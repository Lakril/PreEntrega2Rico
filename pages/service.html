WHAT WE DO

We model and produce highly refined real estate datasets into visual reports and detailed individual property profiles for our customers.


Taking raw data from multiple sources both public and private, then layering the raw data with our own synthesized data to build our corpus, is just one step into creating a high value corpus of data. We don't stop there. We run the corpus through a chain of proprietary processes to further enhance the data.

Service

Data Mining & Software Engineering

Utilizing pattern recognition, we enable our systems to learn from the vast stores of data. By iteratively testing and tuning our systems, the models independently adapt to new real estate data that we harvest.


Machine Learning & AI

Our systems are continually exposed to new real estate data and images. They independently adapt to produce reliable, repeatable decisions and results.


Advanced Statistical Analysis

We apply statistical analysis to determine the shape of the data. This is just another step in our workflow to ensure we understand the dimensions of data for city intelligence applications.


Data Visualization & Information Design

We employ UX and visual design best practices on our data in a way that reduces user's cognitive load. More simply, it's transforming data into knowledge for informed action.
